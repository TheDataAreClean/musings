I have tried to optimise coffee. Better beans, precise ratios, temperature-controlled water, a timer, a scale.

The coffee is better. The ritual is worse. Something about the precision removes the thing that made it a ritual rather than a task.

I still use the scale. But I have stopped caring whether the number is exactly right.
