I just used the [Karnataka State Police app](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/karnataka-state-police/id1358964762), and it’s good?


I have never, and I mean never, had interaction with state infrastructure that has been both positive and smooth. But this time, it took me a total of 30 seconds to get all the things I needed. 30 seconds!


I am surprised and thankful that this experience exists. Is this the Bengaluru tech culture percolating into the state police?
