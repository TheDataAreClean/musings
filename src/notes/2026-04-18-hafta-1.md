Today, I attended the IndieWebClub Bangalore meetup. This is exactly (probably) what I needed at the end of a hard week, a group of people just working on things they care about and writing their hearts away.


Most weeks work just takes over my life, not in volume but definitely in thought. This one was specially hard with the flux and the lack of sleep making it worse. Glad that I am able to step back, and make my world bigger with a bunch of strangers.


Coming here has reminded me of all the things I truly care about — cycling, photography, walking, writing and so much more. I want to turn the focus of my world back to that and not obsess over the things I can’t always control.
