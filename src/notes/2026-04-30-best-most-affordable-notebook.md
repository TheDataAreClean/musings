Bumbling with excitement today, because I am starting a new notebook tomorrow. Not just any notebook, the best notebook (limited) money can buy.

![](/images/uploads/IMG_6587.jpeg "Look at this beauty!")

I have been journaling almost daily for over half a decade now, and it has taken some hit and many trials to reach here. While there are notebooks that offer a [better experience](https://www.leuchtturm1917.us/bullet-journal-edition-2.html), I have never been able to swallow the price.

![](/images/uploads/IMG_6586.jpeg "My trials (with many errors) along the way.")

This [Zafiro Scholar Notebook](https://www.amazon.in/Scholar-Zafiro-Premium-Notebook-Natural/dp/B0977BDG64/) is a tenth of the price. Sure it's slightly thinner, but I can afford to buy many more of them.

I have over 5 more left in my stack, scared that I won't ever find them again. I hope not!
