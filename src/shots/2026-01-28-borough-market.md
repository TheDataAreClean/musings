![Borough Market — stalls under the railway arches, early light, sparse crowd](/images/sample.svg)

Saturday morning, about half eight. The market is open but not yet busy. The stallholders are arranging things, talking to each other, not yet performing for customers.

This is the version of Borough Market that most people never see. By ten it will be shoulder-to-shoulder. At eight-thirty it is just a market.
